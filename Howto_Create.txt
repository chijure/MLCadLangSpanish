To create a language dll for MLCad from scratch load this project into your
visual studio (>= 6.0) and translate text from english to your language in
all string tables, dialogs and menus.

Then compile the dll and replace the XXXX by the name of your language.

If you are ready with translation, inform support@lm-software.com that you
have this dll ready, if you want that your dll is listed at 
http://www.lm-software.com/mlcad
